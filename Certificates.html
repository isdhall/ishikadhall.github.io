CoronaX

A Deep Convolutional Neural Network Model for Reliable Detection of SARS- Related coronavirus (COVID-19)

ABSTRACT

OBJECTIVES

The principal objective of the invention is to provide a software model for efficient and reliable detection of presence of COVID-19 in order to the determine its presence in suspected patients under proper supervision of medical health care professionals.

  • It is beneficial in timely diagnosis of COVID-19 patients for preventing its spread as it has become a pandemic and is spreading at a very rapid rate.
  • It can help in providing accurate number of patients diagnosed with COVID-19 which can be used in medical research.
  • Identifying COVID-19 in early stages of diagnosis can reduce the death rate.

    Provisional Patent filed on April 6, 2020
    Patent Office: India
    Patent application number: 202011015157
